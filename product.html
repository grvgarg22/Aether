about Zeus

Zeus - regain your strength

Zeus is a high functioning, multi action bionic limb combining ease of control with elegant and robust design. It would enable the user to perform everyday tasks right from picking up a pen to holding a dumbbell with confidence. With an option to customise their own grip patterns along with the standard 12, you can not only perform everyday tasks with ease but even conquer the ones specific to your routine.

Get your favourite pattern hydro dipped over Zeus and choose from the 10 different colour options for your life like silicone gloves as well! You are in for a seamless experience with advanced signal processing and wireless gain adjustment in our EMG sensor paired with proportional speed control for any delicate task.

key features

Powerful

One of the highest grip strengths. >150N

Precise

Speed and strength that mirrors your muscle force paired with position aware fingers for a secured grip

Robust

Novel damage resistant fingers without compromising on grip strength

Personalised

Embrace your individuality with a variety of silicone gloves and hydro dipped patterns
