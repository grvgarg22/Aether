f.a.q.

In order to maintain the extended warranties (3 or 5 years), mandatory maintenance must be carried out every 12 months. No additional maintenance is required outside of the warranty period.

Zeus hand inside the hand case along with the service pass mentioning your service record.

Service lifetime for the Zeus hand is 24 months (2 years). Increased service costs are to be expected starting from the 24-month (if extended warranty is not availed within 2 years of purchase) within maintenance because crucial components must be replaced at that time. However if you have availed for extended warranty (3 yrs or 5years), then service will be provided till the exhaustion of 60 months from the date of delivery along with mandatory yearly maintenance. In addition, after the 60th month (5 years), no further repairs will be provided.

Warranty behind from the date of delivery to the authorised prosthetic center.

Free servicing Free loner units Free yearly maintenance (till the warranty lasts years)

If maintenance that is mandatory during the warranty period (see FAQ No. 3) is not performed, Aether may invoice all costs that are incurred due to, or in connection with, the failure to carry out the mandatory maintenance.

The manufacturer grants a grace period for maintenance of no more than one month before or three months after the due date.

To obtain a loaner unit during maintenance or repairs or a trial unit for a trial fitting or marketing purposes, please contact your Aether sales representative or local Aether Customer Service.

The loaner case contains the following components: Zeus hand The trial fittings case contains the following components: Zeus hand Zeus charger Zeus dongle 2 electrodes & electrode cables Please reuse the same packaging for the loaner or trial unit to return all components to Aether inspection.

We recommend wearing an approved Zeus glove to protect the hand from environmental damage. If the user chooses not to wear a glove, the warranty will be void unless reasonable care has been taken to avoid exposure to excessive dust or moisture.

However, the replacement of gaiters, clevis links and palps is free under yearly maintenance (if deemed necessary) during the warranty period (general as well as extended). But these wear and tear parts will be invoiced as usual within normal servicing if requested.
