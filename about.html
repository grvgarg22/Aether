About Aether

Aether Biomedical is a medical robotics company with the goal of making bionics accessible to all. We are driven by the vision of providing precisely tailored solutions for upper limb amputees, supporting you to regain your freedom, independence and true potential. Our aim is to innovate in the field of Biosignal processing with the long-term goal of developing cutting edge prosthetic and rehabilitation devices.

We want to create a dent in medical device innovation by engineering products that exceed global standards without compromising on quality and providing it at best value to the customers. This would be achieved by utilising the latest advancements in technology, from the inception of our ideas at the designing table right to the users we serve all across the globe.

Our Journey

Why?

We came across an upper limb disabled person and while trying to help him build a cheap hand we realised that anything short of a bionic hand would be worthless as it would not enable the user to regain control.

How?

We gathered a team of like minded individuals driven with an idea to bring change in medical device innovation. With more than a dozen iterations over the past 4 years we have now made some thing that we are truly proud of and we hope you would like it as well.

Support and recognition that we received:

Amongst grants we have received Poland Prize, Robot Union and Fasttrack. We also managed to raise funds from Brinc Poland, Joyance, Sunfish and Chiratae Ventures. We were recently nominated by The Economic Times for Startup Awards 2020 in the Best on Campus Category!

Where are we now?

We have received CE certification for our product Zeus.
